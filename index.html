What is an AI-Enabled Digital Knowledge Hub?

An AI-enabled Digital Knowledge Hub is a centralized intelligent platform that integrates a Digital Library, Research Repository, Learning Management System, Institutional Archive and Artificial Intelligence (AI) into one ecosystem.

It enables students, researchers, staff and decision makers to discover, understand, create and share knowledge through AI-powered services.

Vision

It becomes the centre of learning, research, innovation and collaboration, supporting education, governance and national development.
